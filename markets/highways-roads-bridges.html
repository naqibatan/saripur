Transportation

 Highways, Roads & Bridges

HCC is a complete transportation infrastructure solutions provider, having executed all-terrain, all-weather roads and highways with built-in structures such as tunnels, major and minor bridges, cross drainage works, and toll plazas. We have mastered the art of dealing with complex geological conditions ranging from Himalayan terrain to urban agglomerations.

  • HCC has built roads in the remotest of locations featuring extremely challenging climatic conditions as well as in clogged urban settings apart from constructing cross-country highway networks
  • HCC has built more than 4036 lane kilometres of highways spanning the length and breadth of India in the form of Expressways, National Highways, State Highways and Port approach systems
  • HCC has constructed roads under PPP / BOT formats as well as under EPC contracts
  • HCC also executes elevated road corridors & bridges made on design-build basis
