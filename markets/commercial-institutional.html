Industrial & Buildings

 Commercial & Institutional

HCC provides end-to-end construction solutions for all types of Commercial & Institutional buildings. The company’s real estate expertise spans Residential, Commercial and Institutional, covering the complete range of services including core and shell, electrical, HVAC, and finishing works. We specialise in holistic campus development that involves construction of multiple buildings along with support infrastructure, as well as in the development and construction of Green Buildings.

 

Major Projects:

  • Admin Building at RAPP Units 7&8, Rajasthan (Ongoing)
  • Dock Master Complex Building at Naval Dockyard, Mumbai (Ongoing)
  • Training Building at IGCAR, Tamil Nadu (2018)
  • 247Park, Mumbai (2010)
  • Station Buildings at DMRC
  • Station Buildings at Mumbai Metro One (2014)
  • AFNHB Housing Complex at Kharghar, Navi Mumbai (2000)
  • LIC building, Kolkata (1965)
  • Secretariat Building, Chandigarh (1957)
  • Building for RBI at Nagpur (1956)
  • BARC office complex at Tarapur
  • Library Building at Walchand College of Engineering, Maharashtra
