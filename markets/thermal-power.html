Power

 Thermal Power

HCC offers turnkey solutions for both Coal and Gas-based Thermal Power plants. The company has an excellent track record, having executed 18 coal-based projects and 3 gas-based projects.

HCC constructs the entire range of Thermal Power Plant components, including;

  • Turbine Generator Buildings
  • Coal Handling Plant
  • Ash Handling Plant
  • Chimneys
  • Cooling Towers
  • Plant Water Systems
  • Balance of Plant

 

Major Projects:

  • Muzaffarpur Thermal Power Plant, Bihar (2018)
  • Kalol Mehsana Gas Pipeline, Gujarat (2006)
  • Brahmapuram Diesel Power Plant, Kerala (1997)
  • Barauni Thermal Power Plant, Bihar (1982)
  • Trombay Chimney Works, Maharashtra (1956)
