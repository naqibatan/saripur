 Smart Cities & Townships

The fast-paced growth of today calls for smart living, and smart living needs smart cities. With Lavasa, Saripur took its first steps towards building a smart city. Lavasa is a unique urban development project spread over 25,000 acres of land, just 56 kms from Pune. When complete, Lavasa will feature five towns; Dasve, Mugaon, Dhamanohol, Gadle and Sakhri-Wadavli. 

  • Saripur implemented the following at Lavasa;
    • Residential, Commercial, Social and Recreational Infrastructure
    • GIS for City Planning & Management
    • Environment Sensitive Master Plan
    • Robust, Scalable IT Infrastructure
